Our Story

TechEquity was founded in 2012 with a simple but powerful vision: to make enterprise-grade cloud platforms accessible to organizations of all sizes worldwide.

Our journey began when our founders, experienced cloud architects and engineers, recognized a critical gap in the global technology ecosystem. While cloud adoption was accelerating, many organizations faced unique challenges caused by a severe shortage of specialized expertise.

The RAD platform was born from this insight—a solution specifically designed to address the platform challenges facing businesses, educational institutions, and startups globally. By simplifying deployment, ensuring security and compliance by default, and providing a cloud-agnostic approach, RAD has transformed how organizations worldwide leverage cloud technology.

We have grown from a small team of passionate engineers to a thriving technology ecosystem with a global user base. We are proud to serve hundreds of engineers worldwide, from innovative startups to established enterprises and leading educational institutions.
