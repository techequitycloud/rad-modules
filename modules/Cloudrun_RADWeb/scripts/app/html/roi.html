Calculation Assumptions & Methodology




  • Time Savings: RAD reduces deployment time by the configured percentage compared to manual processes.
  • Cloud Cost Savings: Optimized deployments and resource management reduce cloud costs by the configured percentage.
  • Labor Cost Calculation: Based on engineer hourly rate × time saved per deployment × number of deployments
  • ROI Formula: ((Annual Savings - Annual RAD Cost) / Annual RAD Cost) × 100
  • Payback Period: Time required for cumulative savings to equal RAD investment (Monthly RAD Cost / Monthly Total Savings)
  • 3-Year Projection: Cumulative savings over 36 months minus total RAD costs over the same period
