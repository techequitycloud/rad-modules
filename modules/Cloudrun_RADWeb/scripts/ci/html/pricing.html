Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RAD pricing and plans.

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes access to 3 pre-built blueprints, support for a single cloud provider, basic security controls, community support, and up to 5 deployments per month. It's perfect for individuals and small projects looking to get started with RAD.

How does the credit system work?

RAD's credit system allows you to pre-purchase credits that can be used for cloud resource consumption. One credit is approximately equivalent to $0.10 of cloud resources. Credits can be purchased in packages with volume discounts, and you can set budget controls to manage spending. This system provides predictable costs and eliminates surprise bills.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new features and capabilities will be immediately available. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no penalties for changing plans.

Do you offer educational or non-profit discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions, non-profit organizations, and research facilities. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and eligibility for these discounts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money services including digital wallets and regional payment systems. We also support local payment processors to minimize international transaction fees.

Is there a long-term commitment?

No, our monthly plans are billed month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. Annual plans are pre-paid for the year and offer a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. You can cancel your subscription at any time.
